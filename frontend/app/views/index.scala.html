A home for big ideas

In 2016, the Guardian will reopen the Midland Goods Shed at London’s King’s Cross. This will mark the coming together of extraordinary cultural and educational institutions with the Guardian, to create a new kind of civic space.

The building will be a hub for big ideas and conversation, offering events, activities and courses from Guardian Live and institutions we admire, as well as being the home of Guardian Membership.
