Guardian Live events

When we say live element, we really mean it: Guardian Live is our rolling programme of events that includes everything from lectures by some of the world’s foremost thinkers and talkers to exclusive screenings of the latest indy films; from Meet the Journalist sessions at our offices in Kings Place to more intimate, bespoke events. For example, on Saturday 15 October, the Weekend magazine crew are heading west from to Notting Hill for a slap-up brunch with Yotam Ottolenghi and guests (that’s you) at Tabernacle. And later this month, Guardian feature writer Kira Cochrane will be sitting on a comfy sofa at RIBA, chatting to national treasure Clare Balding.

Guardian Space

For the moment, our events will take place at a number of venues around London and the UK. But come January 2016, we'll be flinging open the doors to Space, the new and permanent home for Guardian Live events. Situated just across the road from our offices in King’s Cross, it won't be just a lecture theatre; we want Space to be your space too. A place where you can meet old friends and new, share ideas and great conversations, learn something, expand your mind or just sit back and relax. And if you can't wait until 2016 to see how it’s shaping up, don’t worry; we'll be running regular visits for patrons between now and then.
